The Arab Digital Reform Institute (ADRI) is a start-up New Zealand company, registered with the Companies Office in March 2014 (NZBN 9429041130162). The three employees and shareholders of ADRI are Ali Mazraeh, Ehab Hadi and Rochelle Stewart-Allen. All are Alumni of Victoria University of Wellington. As Chief Executive, Ali Mazraeh undertakes the overall management of the organisation. As Chief Technical Officer, Ehab Hadi is responsible for the technological requirements of the organisation. Rochelle Stewart-Allen as Chief Operating Officer is responsible for the daily operation of ADRI.
